Mehman Ismayilli

Economist & Educator at the University of Oxford

Lecturer in Economics at the University of Oxford. My research interests are industrial organization, experimental economics, and the application of machine learning in economic theory. Previously, I worked at the University of Manchester and the University of Warwick. I hold my PhD in Economics from the University of Leicester.
