Mehman Ismayilli

I am a final year doctoral candidate in Economics at University of Leicester. My research focuses on microeconomic theory, industrial organisation and experimental economics. Particularly, I am interested in horizontal and vertical product differentiations and decision theories under risk.

I am a teaching fellow in Department of Economics at Warwick University and have previously worked as a graduate teaching assistat at the University of Leicester and London Business School. I am an associate fellow of the Higher Education Academy since September 2020.
